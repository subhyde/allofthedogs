Our Return Policy

My Item came damaged, What Do I Do?

All of our items are very carefully packaged. We try our best to get our products to you as safe as possible but mistakes can be made
If you have received a damaged product from us please contact us and we will send one out asap(free of charge)

The sizing doesnt fit, Can I still return the item?

Of course, we understand that sometimes things dont fit just right. We will cover the return shipping costs and offer a discount on your next purchase.

Other reasoning

For whatever reason it may be that you would like to return your purchase. We have a 30 day money back guarantee
Please send us an email via our contact form and we will get back to you within 1-2 business days with more information on how to proceed.
